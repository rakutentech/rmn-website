Rakuten Mindfulness Network

Uplifting work and life of Rakutenians through mindfulness-based approaches

Mission

Rakuten Mindfulness Network is a company supported employee-driven initiative for promoting wellbeing among Rakutenians.

We use scientifically backed mindfulness-based approaches for reaching this goal.

Currently we organize workshops, group meditation sessions, invited talks and various community activities.

Furthermore, RMN contributes to the scientific community through our collaborations with RIT.
